Gloriah SA

About Me

Hello, I'm Gloriah SA, a passionate female musician from Kwa-Thema - Gauteng - South Africa. My journey in music began at a very young age, inspired by the rich cultural heritage and diverse sounds of my homeland. I specialize in Afro-pop and contemporary R&B, Gospel, blending traditional African rhythms with modern production techniques.

With over a decade of experience in the music industry, I've had the privilege of performing at major festivals across Africa and collaborating with renowned artists. My music aims to tell stories of love, resilience, and the African experience.
